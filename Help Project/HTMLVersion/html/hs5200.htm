Setting Default Measurements

Table of Contents Balthisar Software Site Cascade Tips...


 Most of the attributes which utilitze units of measurement allow you to select the unit of measurement to use, such as the one pictured to the left. All such selectable units of measurement have a command in their pop-up menu called Make Default. If you choose this command, then unit of measurement you have selected will be the new default unit of measurement at all times in the future, for that particular attribute.

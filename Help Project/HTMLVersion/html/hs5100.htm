 Some the spin editors accept fractional information to accomodate certain units of measurement such as inches. However, if the unit of measurement you want to use a inherently non-fractional, such a pixels, it's a better idea not to display the fractional portion of the number value.
In spin editors such as these, you can right-click the spin editor to show the contextual menu illustrated below.

 The purpose of the "whole numbers" option is to allow you to toggle the "whole-number" state of the spin editor in question. That way, values that should logically be whole numbers can be entered in a more sensical manner.

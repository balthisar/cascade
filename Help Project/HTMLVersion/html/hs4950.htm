 The HTML Color Picker is divided into two separate, primary panes because depending on the type of color you wish to choose, there are different requirements. The section explains the picker for System color names, illustrated below.
The color picker is composed of two main parts: the composite, or "Current Color" portion on the right, and the available colors list on the left.
As you select colors on the left, a description of the color name appears near the middle of the dialog, and the composite color updates to show you the current color as it appears on your own system.
Because system colors can and do vary from system to system, as well as from platform to platform, ensure that you use them wisely. Their purpose it intended to elegantly integrate portions of your content with the users' own operating systems.
